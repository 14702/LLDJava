Game Flow (sequential)

1
Init Game
initGame(N) creates NxN Board, splits territory A | B
→
2
Add Ships
addShip() validates territory, overlap, places on grid
→
3
Start Game
Validates equal fleets, PlayerA fires first
→
4
Fire Missile
Strategy.nextShot() picks unique coordinate in opponent field
→
5
Hit / Miss
Board.shoot() marks cell HIT or MISS, tracks per-ship hits
→
6
Destroyed?
All cells of a ship hit → ship destroyed
→
7
Winner?
No ships left for opponent → current player wins
